How to Make it Through the Day Without Crying as an Interaction Design Student Living in 2021

Step 1

Affirmation

In the Zoom preview before you join class, look at yourself and repeat some words of affirmation.

Repeat until you begin to believe or your class starts.

Step 2

Travel

Move your workstation to the couch for an exciting change of scenery.

The couch is your vacation home, enjoy it.

Step 3

Cleanse

Delete all the old final documents on your desktop that weren't really the final version.

Enjoy the satisfaction of a clean desktop.

Step 4

Move

Walk the perimeter of your room 12 times.

The outside world is scary, get moving in the space you have.

Step 5

Connect

Turn to Stack Overflow for some much needed socialization.

You are sure to get a few responses.

Step 6

Consume

Eat a bowl of cereal (I don’t know it just helps).

Literally any cereal will do. Even Raisin Bran.

Step 7

Visualize

Imagine yourself in a moment of joy, your code just worked on the first try.

Your code will always run in your dreams.

Step 8

Hope

Buy yourself another pain-prevention computer-sitting technology and let yourself really believe that it will fix your eye, head, back, neck, or butt pain.

Those blue light glasses WILL be the solution.

Step 9

Encouragement

Turn that Raspberry Pi you have lying around into a constant stream of encouragement.
